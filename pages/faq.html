Where does the name krapyDNA come from and what does it mean?

krapyDNA was a meaningless nickname discovered in middle school when I spelled my name, Andy Park, backwards. The meaning and purpose of the name has developed into one of inspiration and hope. Even though we are sinful by nature and have “krapy” DNA, we are not restricted or limited because true strength comes from Christ. With this strength, krapyDNA believes that ordinary people can make an extraordinary impact on the world.

How and when do you release new products?

krapyDNA will release new products in collections. Each collection will have a specific theme and cohesiveness to it. Many companies release these collections in a seasonal format but krapyDNA will release them randomly as ideas for new themes come up. Sign up to our newsletter to stay updated with all new releases!

Will you restock or reprint shirts that are sold out?

Only in rare cases will a shirt be restocked or reprinted. The overall message of krapyDNA will be presented from collection to collection, so once a shirt from an older collection is sold out, it will not be restocked.

How long does it take for my order to arrive?

Domestic orders will take on average 4-6 business days.

International orders will take on average 10-15 business days or more depending on the destination and customs processing.

How much does shipping cost?

Shipping is FREE for all domestic orders.

The cost for international orders will depend on the country and the size of the order. Please note that international orders are subject to regular customs duties and fees levied by the destination country.

What shipping method do you use?

All domestic orders are sent via USPS first class or priority mail.

All international orders are sent via UPS first class International mail.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We gladly accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover) and PayPal as well.

What is your Return Policy?

Our goal here at krapyDNA is for your 100% total customer satisfaction.

Notice must first be given to returns@krapyDNA.com within 2 days of shipment receipt for any exchanges to take place. Unworn shirts can then be exchanged within 10 days of shipment receipt. Please include a copy of the packing slip that was sent with your original purchase. Customers are responsible for the additional shipping charges unless a product is defective in any way.
